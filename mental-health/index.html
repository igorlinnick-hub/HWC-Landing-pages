01 — ExoMind TMS

ExoMind TMS

FDA-cleared, drug-free brain stimulation using advanced ExoTMS™ technology. Gentle magnetic pulses target mood-regulating brain regions — no medication, no sedation, no downtime.

Who It's For

  • Depression that hasn't improved with medications
  • Ongoing anxiety, brain fog, or emotional exhaustion
  • Those who prefer to avoid psychiatric medications

Sessions are under 30 minutes. Most patients feel only a light tapping sensation. No sedation, no recovery time — drive yourself home immediately. Treatment plans typically span 4–6 weeks, 5 days per week.

03 — SGB

Stellate Ganglion Block

A targeted, minimally invasive injection that resets the sympathetic "fight-or-flight" response — rapid relief from PTSD, chronic pain, and migraines. Often effective after just one treatment.

Who Benefits

  • PTSD and trauma survivors — fast-acting relief from hypervigilance
  • First Responders & Veterans with chronic stress reactions
  • Chronic pain patients with neck, shoulder, or arm pain
  • Headache & Migraine sufferers

The Procedure

Using real-time ultrasound imaging, your board-certified anesthesiologist administers a precise local anesthetic in minutes. 30–45 minutes recovery — many patients notice warmth spreading, a sign the block is working.

Results

  • Single SGB often provides weeks to months of relief
  • Series protocol (2–3 blocks) for sustained benefit
  • Low-risk with rare, temporary side effects

04 — Spravato

Esketamine (Spravato)

FDA-approved nasal spray for treatment-resistant depression, administered in our certified clinical setting under full medical supervision. A breakthrough for those who haven't responded to traditional antidepressants.

Who May Be Eligible

  • Adults with treatment-resistant depression
  • Tried multiple antidepressants without improvement
  • Currently under care of a medical provider
  • Meet eligibility after medical evaluation

Treatment Schedule

Initial Phase: Twice weekly for 4 weeks. Maintenance Phase: Reduced frequency based on response. Each visit includes evaluation, nasal spray, and ~2 hour monitored recovery.

Safety

Available only through the certified REMS safety program. All treatments supervised by our licensed psychiatrist. Must be administered in clinic — cannot be taken home.
