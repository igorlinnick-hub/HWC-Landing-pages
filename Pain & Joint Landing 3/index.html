10 — Shockwave

Shockwave Therapy

Non-invasive focused acoustic waves that stimulate natural tissue repair, reduce inflammation, and restore mobility — FDA-cleared and effective for injuries that haven't responded to other treatments.

How It Works

A handheld device emits focused sound waves into injured tissue, triggering biological responses that increase blood flow, promote collagen production, and reduce chronic inflammation. Each 10–20 minute session is performed in-office without anesthesia or downtime.

Conditions Treated

  • Plantar fasciitis and heel spurs
  • Tennis or golfer's elbow
  • Achilles tendinopathy
  • Shoulder and rotator cuff pain
  • Hip bursitis or IT band syndrome
  • Back and neck pain
  • Muscle tightness and scar tissue buildup

What to Expect

  • Session: 10–20 minutes, mild tapping or tingling sensation
  • Recovery: Resume normal activities immediately
  • Plan: 3–6 sessions spaced one week apart

13 — Injections

Regenerative Injections — PRP, A2M, BMAC & Biologics

Our suite of ultrasound-guided regenerative injections use your body's own biologic materials to repair cartilage, tendons, and joints — offering targeted healing with minimal downtime and no surgery required.

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP)

A small blood sample is drawn, concentrated in a centrifuge to isolate platelets rich in growth factors, then injected into the targeted area. PRP stimulates collagen production, repairs tendons and ligaments, and reduces inflammation. Effective for joint pain, arthritis, tendinopathy, and disc injuries.

Alpha-2 Macroglobulin (A2M)

A2M is a large plasma protein that naturally neutralizes the enzymes responsible for cartilage breakdown and chronic joint inflammation. Particularly effective for early arthritis and degenerative joint conditions — protecting the joint while other therapies stimulate repair.

Bone Marrow Aspiration Concentrate (BMAC)

Stem cells derived from your own bone marrow under light sedation for comfort. BMAC contains mesenchymal stem cells that help regenerate damaged cartilage and connective tissue — particularly effective for advanced knee and hip arthritis and post-surgical recovery.

Biologic Cell Therapy

Uses a combination of growth factors and regenerative components from FDA-registered sources to enhance your body's natural healing process. Supports long-term joint function, reduces inflammation, and improves strength and flexibility. All materials are sourced from reputable, FDA-compliant laboratories.

  • Outpatient procedures with minimal downtime
  • Administered by licensed medical professionals
  • FDA-compliant biologic protocols throughout
